About CarDealer

Used car dealer since 1992

Founded in 1992 by Greg Chapman, and now run by his son, James Chapman, CarDealer is a used car showroom featuring one of the widest selection of automobiles in the country. As most businesses, CarDealer was once very small and local. However, as time moved on, and despite some economical struggles in the late 2000s, the showroom grew bigger and bigger, becoming one of the major players in the national market. Nowadays it is much more than just a dealership, namely a fully certified workshop for all major brands. What sets it appart from other showrooms is the passion and dedication of its staff, who are true petrolheads.

"I inherited this business in 2008 after my father retired. That was probably the thoughest moment. I was very new to this, and the financial crisis didn't help. However, when you believe and work hard enough, nothing is impossible. And seeing the size of the company today, compared to what it was ten years ago, the growth we had, not just in the amount of vehicles sold or our income, but also as a group, a team. We have some people we were here since day one, as well as new talent, but we all share a common passion for our job, which is what sets CarDealer apart from the rest. We are not salesmen, we are petrolheads first and foremost."

-James Chapman, CEO
